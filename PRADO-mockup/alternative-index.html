Welcome to PRADO

Please note that the information on European travel and identity documents in this database is not yet complete; several documents and versions of documents are still missing. Please visit us again soon to see if the document you are searching for has been included in our database in the meantime.

Whatever a document's origin, please address any question relating to it to your national contact point. Only there will you be able to obtain extra information (including particulars of foreign documents).

When checking security features of documents: FEEL, LOOK, TILT!
